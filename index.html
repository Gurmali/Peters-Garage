About Us

Peters Garage is a family-owned business based in Kalmar, Sweden, founded by Peter Nilsen after 25 years of working for some of the largest car dealerships in the country. Rest assured Peter, his son, and his brother will be able to diagnose and fix whatever problem you're having with your vehicle.

Whether you're in for a quick service or checkup, waiting for your totaled car to get ready, or you just happened to be in the neighborhood and want to say hi, you are always welcome to enjoy a cup of coffee at Peters Garage.
